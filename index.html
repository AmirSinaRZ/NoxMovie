Description

NoxMovie is an open source and a cross-platform mobile app where you can watch movies, series, seasons, episodes from the movie database JaheshTV. With this app, you can also find season and movie ratings to make a solid decision on what to watch next.

With NoxMovie, you have movies and series information right at your fingertips!

With a lot of features:

  • View movies/series details such as ratings, seasons, similars and other details
  • Search for movies, series and animations
  • Watch movies, series with subtitle or persian dub
